01 / ABOUT

I turn static designs into experiences people remember.

I'm Abdelrahman, a front-end developer who believes the difference between a good site and a great one is almost always in the details of motion — the easing curve on a hover, the timing of a scroll reveal, the little delay that makes an interface feel alive instead of static.

I care about performance as much as polish: animations that run at 60fps, respect prefers-reduced-motion, and never get in the way of the content they're meant to elevate.
